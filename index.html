Terms of service

By creating an account, you agree to use theGargled Hosting for lawful purposes and to keep your account credentials secure. Free-tier accounts are subject to the resource limits stated on your plan page; exceeding them may result in throttling or suspension with notice where possible.

We may suspend accounts without prior notice in cases of active abuse, security compromise, or imminent harm to other users — see the Acceptable Use Policy below for specifics.

Acceptable use policy

The following are not permitted on any plan, free or paid:

  • Hosting malware, phishing pages, or content designed to compromise other systems
  • Sending unsolicited bulk email ("spam") or operating open mail relays
  • Hosting content that exploits or sexualizes minors, in any form
  • Using shared infrastructure for cryptocurrency mining or denial-of-service tooling
  • Impersonating another person, organization, or service for deceptive purposes

Violations are handled through our Report a problem page and reviewed by the abuse & trust team within 48 hours.

Privacy & data handling

We collect the minimum data needed to operate your account: email, billing details for paid plans, and server logs retained for 30 days for abuse investigation. We do not sell or share this data with advertisers or data brokers. Full data retention schedules are available on request.

DMCA & takedown process

Copyright holders may submit takedown notices to abuse@gargled.com. We respond to valid notices within 2 business days and notify the affected account holder, who may submit a counter-notice per standard DMCA procedure.

Data & law enforcement requests

We disclose user data to law enforcement only in response to valid legal process (subpoena, court order, or warrant, as applicable), and we notify the affected user unless legally prohibited from doing so.
